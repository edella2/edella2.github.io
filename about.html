Education and Experiences

I lived and went to school at the University of Illinois at Chicago for Civil Engineering. After graduating I worked for the Illinois Department of Transportation(IDOT). After a couple years working for IDOT as a resident engineer (A fancy way for saying a guy standing around witha clipboard). I got a job working with the engineering design firm Parsons Brinckerhoff where I worked until recently doing drainage design, sign planning, road design, environmental design - well I could go on with this list. I pretty much did every type of engineering design that goes into roads. I decided to make the change from road design to coding recently. It's something I've always been interested in and really feel I can make an impact on the world in this avenue.
